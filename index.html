About Us

We're a student organization dedicated to educating and connecting learners interested in clinical informatics, biomedical data science, and AI applications in healthcare. We host workshops, speaker series, project teams, and study groups with a focus on responsible use of data and reproducible methods.

Our Mission

To foster a collaborative community where students gain practical skills, learn the ethics and limitations of AI in medicine, and build projects that make clinical research and care safer and more effective.

Education

Hands-on workshops and study groups to learn tools and methods.

Collaboration

Multidisciplinary projects that pair students across medicine, CS, and data science.

Ethics

Critical discussions on bias, privacy, and clinical impact.
