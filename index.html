About Us

We're a student organization dedicated to educating and connecting learners interested in clinical informatics, biomedical data science, and AI applications in healthcare. We host workshops, speaker series, project teams, and study groups — all with a focus on responsible use of data and reproducible methods.

Our Mission

To foster a collaborative community where students gain practical skills, learn the ethics and limitations of AI in medicine, and build projects that make clinical research and care safer and more effective.
